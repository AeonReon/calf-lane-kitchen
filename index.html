About Us

More Than a Café — A Place That Brings Coleraine Together

Calf Lane Kitchen isn't just about incredible food (though we've plenty of that). We're a community hub where families, friends, and neighbours come together over properly cooked meals and great coffee.

Every penny spent here goes back into supporting our local community. When you sit down for a fry-up or a Sunday roast with us, you're not just feeding yourself — you're feeding something bigger.

What's On The Menu

Food That Keeps Coleraine Coming Back

From our famous full Irish to the Sunday roast that packs the place every weekend — everything's made fresh and served with a smile.

The Full Irish Breakfast

The one everyone talks about. Bacon, sausages, eggs, beans, toast, and all the trimmings — cooked to perfection. Served until 11:45am. From just £7.50 with coffee.

Sunday Roast Dinner

Our legendary Sunday roast brings families from all over Coleraine. Proper roast meat, fluffy roasties, veg, and gravy that's worth the trip alone. Every Sunday 10am-4pm.

Brunch & Light Bites

Filled bagels, avocado toast with sriracha, sandwiches, and our crispy cod goujons. Plus a kids' menu so the whole family's sorted. Veggie and vegan options available.
